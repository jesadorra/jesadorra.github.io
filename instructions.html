HOW TO PLAY



1. Click a pokémon of your choice. (Fire, Ground, Ice, Rock, Grass)


2. The computer will generate a random pokémon that will be used to fight against you


3. You will lose one (1) health point if your chosen pokémon isn't effective against the chosen pokémon of the computer and vice versa.


4. If your chosen pokémon type is the opponent's weakness, your attack will deal five (5) damage and vice versa.

5. The game will end if either one of you lost all your health points.


HOW TO WIN


To win a match, you must know which element is effective against which element and a huge amount of luck
